MARS Organic

    At the heart of Mars Organic is our commitment to be a living embodiment of love and consciousness in action. We work with thousands of small family farmers in India to cultivate tens of thousands of acres of sustainable organic farmland. All Mars Organic products support health and True Wellness and are made with loving care. Each product is one link in a chain of connectedness between Mother Nature, our farmers and you. By choosing Mars Organic you are completing this chain, actively participating in our mission to create a sustainable environment of True Wellness, providing training and a life of dignity to our farmers, and bringing health, happiness and True Wellness to you. All of our farmers and tribal wildcrafters are educated in organic and biodynamic agricultural practices. We pay all the fees associated with acquiring the necessary organic certifications for them, and then we purchase the harvested crops and herbs at a premium market price. The farmers also rotate between growing crops on their land for Mars Organic with food crops for themselves.



Mission

    The larger goal at Mars Organic is to make food production more sustainable so we can grow safe and healthy to feed everyone in the country. In urban centres, our efforts are focused towards creating a convenient and affordable service for delivering fresh, safe and healthy food straight from the farm to your plate. On the other end, for rural development, we want farming to regain the respect it once had and become a profitable livelihood option.

    We believe that everyone has the right to safe food that is grown with lots of love and care. We encourage people to know more about where their food comes from, who grows it and how.

    At Mars Organic, we are always looking for opportunities to collaborate and extend our Mars Organic network of farmers to include new and varied organic and/or sustainable produce.
